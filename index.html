WheelHub Web Project

This is a team project consisting of three students. Here's an overview of the wheelHub project. WheelHub is a collaborative team project developed by a leading automotive retailer, which has been a pioneer since 1995. The launch of its state-of-the-art website marks a significant expansion in its innovation. Renowned for its high-quality cars and accessories across the US, WheelHub's new online platform aims to redefine the automotive shopping experience. The website features a virtual showroom that highlights WheelHub's commitment to performance and style, catering for a range of needs from sedans to SUVs. The site includes five main pages: Home, Promotions, Registration, All Cars and About Us, each designed for clarity and engagement. Fully responsive, the website ensures a seamless experience across desktops, tablets and smartphones, providing a consistent and enjoyable user experience on any device.
